For the User:

How is the app used?

  1. Open App
    1. The default "home" screen should be displaying your camera
  2. Point your phone camera at a mural
      3. Make sure that the entire (or at least a majority) of the mural can be seen inside the view shown on your phone screen
  3. Click the capture button (the round button at the bottom of the screen)
  4. An information page should then pop up
    1. If information page does not appear, then either:
      1. Try clicking the capture button again
      2. Adjust your location, and then click the button again
      3. Adjust the angle at which you are pointing your phone at the mural
  5. You should be able to scroll down the information page to view more (if needed)
  6. To exit this information page, swipe down
  7. To view information on another mural, point the camera at the new mural and click the round button. Repeat the above steps

Other features located on the information page

  • Tap the headphones icon to listen to the auditory version of the text
  • Tap the artist's name to see their biography and:
    • Other murals they have done
    • Background info (such as their inspiration, why they got involved, etc.)
      • Possibly even a contact us section (helps the artist and people looking to hire them. Gives them incentive to help us w/ mural descriptions etc)
  • Next to the artist pic + name will be a rating showing how popular the current mural is (has yet to be implemented)
    • User can press little flame icon to show that they "like" it (maybe change this)
  • To exit a mural page or artist bio page, swipe down

Other features located on the Home page

  • Tap the explore button (to the right of the camera button) to view the general history of the park
    • Tap on each of the events in the timeline to see more about each section
    • Tap on the back arrow button on the top left to go back to the timeline
    • Swipe left or right to travel along the timeline
  • Swipe down to return to the home page
