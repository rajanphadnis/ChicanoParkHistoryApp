For the User:

  • Open App
  • Point camera at mural
  • Click round button at bottm
  • Information page should come up
  • Scroll to view more
  • Tap headphones to listen to description
  • Tap artist name to see:
    • Bio
    • Other murals they have worked on
    • Background info (inspiration, etc.)
      • Possibly even a contact us section (helps the artist and people lookng to hire them. Gives them incentive to help us w/ mural descriptions, etc.)
    • Next to the artist pic + name, rating of how popular current mural is (has this been implemented yet?)
      • User can press little flame icon to show that they "like" it (maybe change this)
  • To exit a mural page or artist bio page, swipe down
  • To see the general history of the park, tap the bottom explore button on the right
    • Tap on each of the events in the timeline to see more about each section
    • Tap on the back arrow button on the top left to go back to the timeline
    • Swipe left or right to travel along the timeline
